PON HR Refresh – Germany Consumer HR

Outbound Calls program to drive KYC refresh and reduce suspension or cancellation risk.

Overview

We focus on Germany’s Consumer HR segment. Premium customers are prioritized. OB Calls are executed prior to suspension and a robust partial-refresh path maintains basic status when documentation is provided.

  • Refined recipient lists and enriched data for effectiveness.
  • Portfolio Manager POC used for the premium base.
  • HR to HR Jurisdiction: +30 days, GSG OB calls, and additional SOW requirements.
  • Process is running smoothly and reused by other markets.

Overview - Outbound Calls HR Strategy

Workflow Optimization & Oversight

  • Integration of multiple data sources - Spend, Tenure, Centurion inventory, HRJ CASES, Credit Active, Case Age - into a single workflow, enabling precise prioritization and minimizing manual effort.
  • Automated validation rules and quality checks to prevent errors and ensure compliance before outreach.
  • Harmonized process design across markets and ensuring a consistent customer experience.
  • Dynamic sub-process for retrieving missing account information, reducing cancellations and maintaining BAU alignment.
  • Real-time tracking of batch progress and updates from Concentrix, enabling timely follow-ups and accountability.

Performance Management

  • SLA-driven weekly outbound cycles with automated reporting on contact rate, completion rate, and time-to-resolution.
  • Performance dashboards updated in real time, providing visibility for KYC and Concentrix teams.
  • Established performance framework including contact rate, completion rate, and time-to-resolution, supported by transparent reporting.
  • Defined escalation channels for exceptions such as Centurion file, CEN file and HR–HRJ transitions.

Customer Engagement & Experience Quality

  • Scripted call flows aligned with DE/AT regulations, ensuring clear communication and reducing escalations.
  • 21-day outreach cycle optimized for higher engagement and completion rates, monitored via workflow analytics.
  • Feedback integration loop from Concentrix to refine scripts and approach based on observed outcomes.

Risk Mitigation & Compliance Strengthening

  • Early identification of at-risk accounts (Day-60) with automated alerts and escalation triggers.
  • End-to-end tracking of missing documentation, improving audit-readiness and compliance oversight.
  • Close alignment with Compliance and KYC Team for manual overrides and sensitive cases requiring additional due diligence.

Cross-Functional Alignment

  • Shared dashboards and standardized reporting enabling fast decision-making across CEN, TLS, Business, MCO and Concentrix teams.
  • Collaborative escalation process for exceptions (Centurion, CEN files, HR–HRJ transitions), documented for transparency.
  • Weekly syncs and continuous feedback loops with DE/AT partners to adjust priorities and maintain operational consistency.

  • High Risk portfolio: approx. 5,600 AIF YTD (DE: 5k / AT: 0.6k)

  • Since 03/2024: HR process in BAU. As of today: 56% refreshed, 24% in progress. Remains 20% still to be contacted

Overview – Germany & Austria • Consumer HR Outbound Calling

  • Weekly calling cycle with capacity of up to 60 outbound calls per week.
  • When weekly volumes exceed capacity, prioritization is based on customer spend.
  • Centurion accounts excluded – managed by TLS via shared tracking file.
  • HRJ cases not included – handled directly by CEN.
  • Platinum Plus Cards removed from scope to keep focus on core Consumer HR accounts.
  • Clear segmentation & refined recipient lists for targeted actions.
  • Stable and scalable operating model supporting DE and AT HR processes.
  • Designed to strengthen risk management and improve documentation compliance.
  • Process running smoothly and reused by other markets (France, UK).

Operational cadence

  • We call selected customers in the suspension warning and again at suspension confirmation.
  • 3 calls per client every 21 days until resolution.

Use Spend prioritization whenever the weekly volume exceeds capacity.

Enhancements since Jan 2025

  • OB Calls in suspension windows: we call customers at warning and at confirmation, executing a cadence of 3 calls per client every 21 days.
  • Partial-refresh path (since 03/25): Implemented after detecting many cases with Basic completed but missing Supplementary. A targeted partial-cancellation flow cancels only the supplementary component, supported by updated comms and business rules.
  • HR → HRJ adjustment: +30 days extension and GSG OB calls.
  • Reinstatement workflow: for documentation arriving by post after the effective cancellation, ensuring fair recovery of accounts affected by postal delays or internal processing errors.

Next challenges

  • AT Suspension Retrieval & 2026 Documentation Framework: mapped end-to-end AT suspension to identify operational and compliance dependencies. Designed a non‑suspension‑based set‑list to keep outreach and collection regardless of status, with targeted comms and follow‑ups to maintain return rates without suspension triggers.
  • Incorporate Learnings into iRefresh – DE/AT Expansion: request to implement iRefresh for Germany and Austria across Low and Medium risk, covering a larger base with a team of 8 Concentrix agents making weekly OB calls. Spend prioritization essential to manage volume. Execution relies on raw business data and continuous refinement for a scalable, data‑driven framework.
  • Prepare for 2027 SOW/SOF requirements.
